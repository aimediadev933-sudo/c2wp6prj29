Project Overview

Mission

Transform small and mid-sized cable TV operators into key players in bridging the digital divide, particularly in underserved and remote communities of the Philippines.

Approach

Establish open, software-defined Internet Exchange Points (IXPs) and deploy community-based wireless and fiber-optic networks through deep community engagement and civic partnerships.

Target Communities

  • Binangonan (Rizal Province)
  • Science City of Muñoz (Nueva Ecija Province)
  • Surrounding barangays

Project Activities

Infrastructure Deployment

  • Software-defined IXPs (SCIMIX and CONNECT-IX)
  • 26 km fiber backbone installation
  • 8 km drop cable deployment
  • 35 public WiFi access points

Capacity Building

  • BGP training for cable operators
  • SDN training programs
  • Community tech contact training
  • APNIC membership applications

Community Engagement

  • Community-led fiber installation
  • Partnership with Rotary Club
  • Local government collaboration
  • Telehealth and e-learning facilities

Academic Integration

  • Eduroam-enabled public nodes
  • Research connectivity support
  • University partnerships
  • Best paper awards and publications
